Foster Care Relocation

Foster care children often undergo multiple relocations and change schools, resulting in losing of a sense of belonging and community

This project strives to address this problem by providing an interface where relocations can be made within the same school district, preserving the child's community. Social workers can select individual cases or place siblings together and make better decisions based on age, location and special needs.

Highlights

Move away from paper based workflow to web based workflow

Ability to configure parameters like special needs and medical conditions for each case

Preservation of the community and avoidance of lost years in school

Technologies Used
