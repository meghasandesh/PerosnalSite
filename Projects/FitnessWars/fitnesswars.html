Fitness Wars

As part of my coursework in Human-Computer Interaction, I worked with a team of 4 people to conceptualize and build an application which helps people maintain a better fitness regimen.

After conducting an initial survey of what people in the aged 18-35 years old (the target group for the system) thought was most important, it was found that motivation between workouts and a way to work out with friends were most important

We came up with a two pronged solution to address these needs and innovate - a web app+mobile app

The mobile app is intended to be used everyday by the user to keep track of his workouts and schedule workouts. In addition, he is able to track where his friend is, so that friends can co-ordinate and work out together.

The web app is to be used to manage the overall fitness regimen and keep track of overall team progress. Also, users can sign up for individual and group challenges and get rewarded for it.

Highlights

6 of 7 users tested were excited to use and recommend the system

Social network integration

Challenge/Reward System - Individual and group

Progress Tracker

Workout Scheduler and push notifications for mobile app

Potential for sponsorship
