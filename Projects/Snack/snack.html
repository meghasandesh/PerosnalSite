Snack

How do you help health advisors manage a large clientele and optimize their workflow?

Emory hospital employs health advisors to advise Emory employees on general health and fitness. Even though most of the work is done on computers, the advsiors find it hard to respond to the large number of clients and keep up with administrative tasks like data logging. With Snack, we automate much of the administrative tasks, while providing a unified interface for all tasks.
