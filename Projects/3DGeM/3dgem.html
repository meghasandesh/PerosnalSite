3D GeM

3D GeM is an interactive system which employs natural interactions and gestures in a 3D environment to interact with computer systems. The aim of this systems is to place more emphasis on the task at hand and the easiest way to achieve it as opposed to the details of how to do it on a computer.

In this project, we have created a virtual bookshelf which can be used to open and read books in a home or office environment.

Gestures Supported

Hovering over books to make a selection - a selected book pops out

Grab a book to confirm selection

Move book up and down

Open/close book
